Experience

This is not like any other book for me. It is a fact that my family have spend a laaaaaarge sum amount of money to make me earn a degree in engineering. I completed my Undergraduate in Electronics and Communication with very little knowledge if at all anybody can sympathize on it, but with a hell lot of questions lingering in mind creating a deep hollow hell hole craving for actual knowledge. Luckily, my father seems to be a studious guy in his University days (those days really did atleast seem to have some stuff for knowledge), and i started reading the books he got as winning prizes in his days. One of them is Entertaining Electronics book. Eventhough, this book was lying around for 22 years i have not given much important to it.

Just finishing my Undergraduate with my love for comics, this book took me to peaks of surprise ! My brain got hit by storm of cleaning from this single book which not even a book in my college have provided me. I really tried to learn from them - what is the use if fundas are broken with me ? So Yes, this is one of those awesome books which give us the travel experience by which we will not even feel time going by. I guess lot of my learning on comics also inspired by this book.
