2012 Economix: How and Why Our Economy Works (and Doesn't Work) Michael Goodwin, Dan E. Burr, Joel Bakan, David Bach Abrams ComicArts

What i enjoyed !

In short - i love comics. I am learning economics, thus it is a beautiful combination of those - to get the most of economics in a enjoyable language that almost everyone loves... comics. Just go through the book in your leisure and one can see that it is easier to trace history in authors words. Although this is not a comprehensive collection of all historical events in economic history, but consists of sheer amount of information about economics, history, social issues we face to a large extent. That kind of bootstrap will enable one to easily to continue further.

It is sad that this book is not available with COPYLEFT license, and fortunately my nearby library retained a copy.
