WHAT IS THIS ABOUT ?

Cooperatives have long existed probably before our time now, and will surely exist even after our death. That is a fact. This writeup is not the cooperative that big business form during convergence and conglomeration for the sake for maximizing profit, deepening power establishment, exploitation, etc... This is about who a bunch of individuals of similar thought process, ideas, skill sets, can mutually agree upon a fundamental set of concrete principles that does not leverage on competition and profit theories, but on mutual wellbeing, reciprocity, collaboration, sharing, transparency, education, social solidarity, etc...

However, i am not going to discuss with you about what cooperatives are, its theory, etc... and a generic historical awareness can be provided by wikipedia page. What i am going to discuss is how individuals and their networks could form a effective cooperative with whay they have in hand. In the era of Information & Communication technology, form of goods and services are shifting rapidly from physical realm to digital realm. What i have learned is Cooperative philosophy does not provide you a set of success formulas that pushes you in to the sea of hurricanes of business competition, and expect you to succeed, so that a new formula would arise out of your effort. What it provides is a basic set of principles and guidelines that evolves from pragmatic understanding how we human beings can really live as social beings by not falling into the trap of competition and greed.

THE TRIUMPH

Wherever i see now, there is a community for everything (especially in tech. world). Not more than 10 years ago, there is barely a community and even if it is there it would be around a set of philosophical background and basically serve mutual learning platform - which we usually term as clubs. Fast forward 10 years, there is literally a community for every programming language, every technological framework, for every skill. This is the case in Urban places, where as in rural areas it is still challenging to have a community apart from local administrative councils. Communities are becoming the great place of knowledge sharing & deliberation that spans from technological issues to political issues. People in communities have more networked awareness about things that are happening around, that atleast enables forming a mindset to filter out the noise that we are constantly bogged down with.

What if these Networked individuals, who are already grouped together either for sharing knowledge or actively involved in addresing social issues, -- could use their skills, tools, knowledge to evolve from communities to networked community-cooperatives ?

  • What if they can use the collective effort to address social issues more effectively ?
  • What if such effort enable them to sustain themselves economically and financially ?
  • What if such a triumph enables them to live independently - off the corporate or paternal bureaucratic systems ?
  • What if they represent the living examples and proofs of alternatives ?

Already many communities are successful and stand out as roll models in civil society forms, atleast with the ones i am in touch with. They have even participation at times associated with social issue. What i am seeking is a further evolution in communities that would use individual skillsets and technologies for addressing social issues or to provide a service for marginalized people and sectors, with their collaborative nature.

SEARCH FOR ALTERNATIVE

We know for sure, that individuals health and happiness are significantly tangled with socio-economic conditions. Thus searching for alternative is a normative social function, which is when an individual's awareness about the systems and knowledge around them comes into picture. Without deliberation and analysis, empathizing on opinions and ideas would be difficult.

Those who understand the tangled interconnections between social, political, economical, cultural systems & practices, polices, decisions does always influences other creating a complex system where nothing can be deterministic. But the search for alternative that accept the non-deterministic and organic nature of systems and their associations always has continued and will continue. Alternatives, always has triggered the historical changes which in turn makes history. Without alternative ideas and systems, we would not be here. There is always a frustration potential behind a innovation, a degree of hatred about the state of conditions and things around the one who strives to bring the change through that innovation. BOOT A COOP

Invoking a Cooperative practice often is not similar to what a capitalistic regime would do. Unlike, the capitalist model of cooperativeness, it is harnessed at the decentralized grass-roots level. The function of cooperative action differ completely in both theories. Capitalism contradicts itself with competition and thus uses cooperative structure to monopolize the market under one operation. Thus cooperative strategy can either be used for progressive or regressive outcome. It usually involves taking care of process to achieve the goal of cooperation. Not only the goal is vital, but also how the process is deviced and practiced in a cooperative way is vital.

Cooperativism as an alternative to crisis redundant, vicious Capitalism which devour well being and environment required for sustaining human life seems attractive. But one has to understand why Capitalism is crumbling around us since its inception. As Joseph Stiglitz stated, it was built on numerous theoretical assumptions upon assumptions that construct the fundamental foundation of the system. This construction eventually struggles, defends, and often gets offensive to stabilize itself from criticism and attacks. Since it is a form and function that gets infected in all agents who uses capitalism to derive economic support for life, will eventually become the vangaurds of its defense. We also have to accept the fact, that we are in this situation for which capitalism has contributed much in our past.

I am learning some of the properties that help construct a pragmatic alternative for such centrally controlled, secretive, exploiting, phase of our economic history. A democratic nation most possibly would support the right to form a cooperative association, devoid of gender difference. For instance, in our country - India, it has become a fundamental right to initiate or be a part of a cooperative or union. This supports the constitutional preamble of economic democracy. Whether we people choose to adapt and function is the pragmatic question. While the established economic functioning is mostly mixed and defined by free market, state intervention, & financial institutions, it is necessary to know about national, regional and local governance, socio-economic conditions to form a rational cooperative association. This means there is no formula as usually chased for success in modern capitalistic economy. Its rigor gets more organic and influenced by local conditions rather than global and distant conditions.
